Broke Man's Ramen

Description

Broke Man's Ramen is a recipe I came up with on a chilly winter night when I didn't have much time or energy or money to make a "proper" dish. It is a simple, but delicious, take on dressing up instant ramen to make it more filling and more palatable. Can easily feed a family of five!

Ingredients

  • 3 Packs of Maruchan ramen
  • Chicken Broth
  • Chicken Stock
  • Water
  • One Half of a Decent Size White Onion
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • One Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Ground Ginger
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
